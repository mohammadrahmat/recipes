Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 2 teaspoons cooking oil
 - 1/2 teaspoons salt
 - 1/2 teaspoons pepper
 - nicely chopped onion
 - nicely chopped tomatoes

Directions:
 1. Add all ingredients to a bowl and mix them thoroughly.
 
 2. Fry the new paste in a frying pan for few minutes until the paste is solid enough.
 
 3. Serve and enjoy.
